# Privacy Policy for Obedience

**Last updated:** 27 April 2026

This policy explains what information Obedience ("the app", "we", "our") collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. Obedience is published by Aidan Hyland ("we"), based in the United Kingdom. If you have any questions about this policy you can contact us at obedienceapplication@gmail.com.

## Summary

Obedience is a local-first app. By default, everything you create — your prayer rule, daily checks, workout history, walks, push-ups, bookmarks — is stored only on your device. We do not run analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking inside the app. Some features (cloud backup, account-based password reset) are optional and clearly require an account. Location data is only used while you are actively tracking a walk or run.

## What information we collect

### Stored only on your device

The following data is created and stored locally on your device, in the app's own storage. It does not leave your device unless you opt in to cloud backup (see below).

- Daily rule check-ins, custom prayer rules, personal intercessions, occasional prayers
- Strength training sessions, sets, reps, weights, and program configuration
- Walks and runs, including distance, pace, and route samples used to compute them
- Push-up counter sessions and history
- Quick HIIT workouts and saved workouts
- Bible bookmarks, scheduled notifications, app settings and preferences

### Collected only if you create an account

Account creation is optional. If you choose to create one for cloud backup or password recovery, we collect:

- Your email address
- An encrypted password (handled by our hosting provider — see below — and never visible to us in plain text)

### Collected only if you enable cloud backup

Cloud backup is optional and off until you create an account and explicitly use it. When enabled, the app uploads a single snapshot of the data listed above ("Stored only on your device") to your account. The most recent snapshot replaces the previous one. If you delete your account, the snapshot is removed.

### Collected only while tracking a walk or run

The Walk and Run tracker uses your device's location services to measure distance and pace. Location data is processed on your device. It is included in your saved walk/run history (alongside your other local data). Location samples are not transmitted to any third party. If cloud backup is enabled, your saved walk/run summaries (distance, pace, time) are included in the backup snapshot like any other data.

You can revoke location permission at any time in your device settings.

### What we do not collect

- We do not run analytics or telemetry. We do not record which screens you visit, what you tap, or how often you open the app.
- We do not use advertising SDKs or ad identifiers.
- We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or data brokers.
- We do not collect your contacts, photos, microphone audio, or browsing history.

## Third parties we share data with

The only third party that holds your data is **Supabase** (https://supabase.com), our hosting and database provider. Supabase stores:

- Your email and authentication record (only if you create an account)
- Your most recent backup snapshot (only if you enable cloud backup)

Supabase processes this data on our behalf as a data processor. Their privacy policy is available at https://supabase.com/privacy. Servers are located in West EU (Ireland).

We do not share your data with any other third party.

## How long we keep your data

- **Local data on your device:** kept until you delete it from within the app or uninstall the app.
- **Account and cloud backup data:** kept until you sign out (sign-out keeps the data on the server) or delete your account (which removes both the account record and the backup snapshot).

## Your rights and controls

You can:

- **See what is included in a backup** in Settings → Cloud Backup.
- **Disable automatic cloud backup** at any time using the toggle in Settings → Cloud Backup.
- **Manually back up or restore** from Settings → Cloud Backup.
- **Sign out** of your account, which keeps the snapshot on the server but disconnects your device.
- **Delete your account**, which removes both the account and the cloud backup snapshot. This is available in Settings → Cloud Backup → Delete Account.
- **Revoke location, notification, or other system permissions** at any time from your device settings.
- **Request a copy of your data** by contacting obedienceapplication@gmail.com. Because the app stores everything locally, the most complete copy of your data is the one already on your device.

If you are based in the EU/UK, you also have rights under the GDPR / UK GDPR including the right to access, correct, restrict, and object to processing of your personal data, and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

## Children

Obedience is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us at obedienceapplication@gmail.com so we can remove it.

## Security

Account credentials and cloud backup data are transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS) and stored using Supabase's built-in encryption-at-rest. Despite reasonable safeguards, no system is perfectly secure. If we become aware of a security incident affecting your data, we will notify affected users in accordance with applicable law.

## Changes to this policy

If we make a meaningful change to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify users in the app or by email. Your continued use of the app after a change indicates that you accept the updated policy.
